About Us

Kingjames Agro-produce enterprise is a registered Agricultural produce marketing firm. We provide an answer to the missing link between farmers and the end users of these farm produce, and ultimately we provide answer to the question; everybody is farming, who is marketing what is being produced at the right price? We therefore provide a direct link between farmers and industries that make use of farm produce. Our focus We Locate and propose potential business deals by contacting potential partners; discovering and exploring opportunities We answer these questions Are you a farmer and you are looking for off takers for your agricultural produce? Are you an agro-allied producer and seek for customers for your product or Produce? Are you looking for whom to brand and market your produce at the right price? Relax and let us market for you.
